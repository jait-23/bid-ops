Bid Opportunities

CalPERS has a variety of opportunities for you and your company to become a business partner. If you're interested in doing business with us, you must register for an account first. Once you've registered and logged in, you can view or print bid documents by adding them to your Bid List. After you've added a bid to your Bid List, you'll receive email notifications about the bid opportunity. Adding a bid to your account doesn't require you to submit a bid proposal to CalPERS.
